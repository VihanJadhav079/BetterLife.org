About Us: At BetterLife, our mission is to make life more organized and help people discover their purpose and direction. We aim to guide individuals to make informed decisions in emotional wellness, career planning, and financial management. Our goal is to empower every visitor with tools and insights to lead a more structured and fulfilling life. Created by Vihan Jadhav and Tanmay Gupta, we are dedicated to creating a space where growth and self-improvement are accessible to all, and where every decision you make moves you closer to your ideal life path.
